Jurassic Newsletter - 2020-11-01

Full Capacity

Dear current and potential new clients. During these difficult times, we are sad to announce that Jurassic Mapping will not be taking on any new clients or additional projects from existing clients for the next few months. Nothing makes us more happy than to have new and existing customers reach out to us with new projects to work on, however, there sometimes comes a point when you take on too many tasks that all of them collectively suffer. At Jurassic Mapping it is important to us that each and every project recieves the time it requires to achieve nothing less than the quality you have all come to expect from our company. Please be on the lookout for another newsletter update in the future indicatiing when we will be accepting new projects once again!
