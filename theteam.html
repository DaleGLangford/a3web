Meet the Team

Dr. Dinosaur D. Things

Co-Founder, CEO

Dinosaur D. Things grew up on an isolated the western side of Kauai Island, Hawaii in the city of Walmea. There on the tiny island he was always intrigued by the bigger world, what was out there, and what it had to offer. Dinosaur left Kauai for university and began his studies at Hazard University. It was there that Dinosaur realized how data and information can change the world. Dinosaur began his career in GIS soon after that and worked under many mentors until he started Jurassic Mapping five years ago. He hopes to help others discover the stories the world has to offer through maps and GIS.
