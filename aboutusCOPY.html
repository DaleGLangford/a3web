Who We Are.

We are Jurassic Mapping, a collective of enthusiastic GIS wielding dinosaurs with a hunger to help you achieve your mapping needs. Our story is simple and humble, beginning with a dream of one day bringing new and innovative mapping solutions to your business and community. Today we can officially say that we have taken the many important steps in realizing that dream. We do not want to stop there though and continue to push the envelope is mapping solutions and innovation.

What We Do.

What do we do? Simple. We provide the solutions you need, when you need them, and in-line with your company goals and values. All our mapping projects start with an in-depth assessment and discussion with you the client to determine exactly what you need. Whether small scale web-based mapping or company wide GIS integration, Jurassic Mapping will get it done

How We Do It.

Once we have determined what it is your company needs, we set out in designing a workflow that suits you. Our specialists maintain constant communication with our clients throughout the process to ensure that the best technology and techniques are used. We want you to feel like you have had a hand in the production and can apply your own stamp of approval.
