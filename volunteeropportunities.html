Digitizing the Old Maps

Digitizing in GIS is the process of converting geographic data either from a hardcopy or a scanned image into vector data by tracing the features. It leads to every feature having a proper locational identity. Here at Jurassic Mapping, you can come volunteer to help us in digitizing the maps for our valuable clients. You can have the opportunity to work with our amazing dinosaur-obsessed team.

Data Collection

We have an ongoing project for our clients at Fictional Tree Group Ltd. which includes collecting the tree information such as tree species, accuracy of position, tree height and health status. So all the tree-huggers out there, you can help us gather more information. We can use as much information as there it is available for us to provide our clients with the best services.
