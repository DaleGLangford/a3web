Digitizing the Old Maps

Digitizing in GIS is the process of converting geographic data either from a hardcopy or a scanned image into vector data by tracing the features. It leads to every feature having a proper locational identity. Here at Jurassic Mapping, you can come volunteer to help us in digitizing the maps for our valuable clients. You can have the opportunity to work with our amazing dinosaur-obsessed team.

Data Collection

We have an ongoing project for our clients at Fictional Tree Group Ltd. which includes collecting the tree information such as tree species, accuracy of position, tree height and health status. So all the tree-huggers out there, you can help us gather more information. We can use as much information as there is available for us to provide our clients with the best services.

Events and Conferences

We here at Jurassic Mapping love to host events and conferences. Anybody who wants can attend these events. You can volunteer to help us organize these events which includes several phases - Creating an organizing structure- putting together a group of people who are going to run the conference, and planning the ways they will work together. It gives you a chance to have a closer look at what goes on behind the scenes at our company and also you get to meet people who have been working in the field for long. Hence, helping you to learn so many things and make contacts.
